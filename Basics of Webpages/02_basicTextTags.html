The final type of list is a detailed list. A detailed list works a little differently than the rest. In a detailed list, you give each "list" tag a title. This may be useful instead of each list having 1, 2, 3, 4, etc. in front. This would allow you do do things like: Step 1, Step 2, Step 3, etc.

(write your description)
(write /dd with arrows around it)

repeat step 2
repeat step 3
repeat step 4 until you have as many entries as you need
